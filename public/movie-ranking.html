Best Movies According to Chirag

My top 3 movies of all time that you should watch.

Intersellar

I love this movie because of how detailed and scientifically interesting this is with complex ideas being played around.Most importantly, it tells us the importance of time through time dilation.

Predestination

Another complex movie that totally confuses you but when you realise the actuality of what's happening, it absolutely blows your mind.

Avengers:Endgame

This was an end to a saga which everybody was waiting for and it did not fail to deliver.
It had action, suspense and a nice finish which everybody had wished for.
